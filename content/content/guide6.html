This week, we’ll move on from insurance markets and into physician treatment decisions. Just as asymmetric information between patients and insurance companies was a big part of the adverse selection problem, this module will show how asymmetric information between physicians and patients can also create some interesting dynamics and incentives.

Objectives for this week

  • Explain the principal-agent problem in the context of medical care
  • Solve for treatment levels when physicians set prices and quantity

Activities

  • In-class problem on physician agency. The worksheet is here.
  • Weekly check-in on Friday
